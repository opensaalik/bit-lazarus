implement this for local regtest:: Plan: Auto-Signing Receipts + 30% Collateral Bond. 
The Design
When the payer's WebTorrent client verifies a piece, the frontend automatically signs a receipt with the payer's wallet and submits it to the server -- no user interaction, no "confirm" button. The payer's bond is 30% of the reward, making it painful to cheat even if they modify the client.
Economics example:
Reward: 10,000 sats (hunter gets this on success)
Payer bond: 3,000 sats (30%)
Hunter bond: 3,000 sats (30%)
If payer freeloads and gets caught → loses 3,000 sats
If hunter abandons → loses 3,000 sats
